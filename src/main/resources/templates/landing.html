Welcome to Garden Pulse.



Garden Pulse is your one-stop shop for recording and managing gardens and plants. You can also connect with friends, and checkout each others gardens, or create tags to view gardens that you are interested in. To get started, please register with the link above. Once you have registered, you will be prompted to verify your email address, after which you will be able to log in to the application and begin your gardening journey. This application is under active development by team 400 of SENG302 at the University of Canterbury.
